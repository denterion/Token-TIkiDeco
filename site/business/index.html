Why Hospitality Needs Transparent Loyalty

Eligibility clarity
Published rules and snapshot blocks can reduce confusion around limited pilot eligibility.
Operational trust
Reports and hashes can help show when rules, outcomes, or corrections were published.
Guest experience

On-chain

Off-chain

Boundary
