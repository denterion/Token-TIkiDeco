# Value And Utility Boundary

Status: public-communications boundary. This document is not legal, financial, tax, or investment advice.

TikiDeco / TIDE is an Ethereum Sepolia prototype. TIDE is not offered for sale, has no stated monetary value, is not deployed on mainnet, and has not completed an independent audit.

## Utility

Utility means a limited operational use inside a defined process, such as eligibility review, report verification, or pilot RSVP handling.

For TikiDeco, utility is currently conceptual or planned. Any future hospitality pilot must be conditional, limited, documented, manually reviewed, and subject to legal, operational, privacy, and security constraints.

Utility does not mean:

- cash value;
- exchange value;
- ownership rights;
- revenue rights;
- not guaranteed access;
- not guaranteed benefit;
- mainnet readiness.

## Market Price

Market price means a public or private trading price for an asset. TIDE currently has no stated monetary value, no approved market, no exchange listing claim, and no mainnet deployment.

The project must not publish, imply, or encourage a TIDE price.

## Financial Value

Financial value means an unsupported claim that TIDE can be bought, sold, redeemed, exchanged, used for cash-equivalent benefit, or expected to produce financial return.

No repository fact currently supports a financial value claim for TIDE.

## Business Value

Business value means operational usefulness for the project, such as clearer eligibility records, less manual ambiguity, report integrity, and community feedback.

Business value is not the same as token holder financial value. A business workflow may be useful to the operator while TIDE still has no stated monetary value and no financial rights.

## Why TIDE Does Not Currently Have Stated Monetary Value

- It is deployed only on Ethereum Sepolia.
- It is not offered for sale.
- It is not deployed on mainnet.
- It has no approved redemption path.
- It has no approved exchange listing.
- It does not provide equity, debt, revenue, property, hotel ownership, dividend, profit, or return rights.
- Independent audit has not started.
- Future utility remains planned or conceptual unless separately verified.

## Evidence Required Before Any Future Value-Related Claim

Before any value-related statement can be considered, the project would need separate evidence and approvals, including:

- external counsel review;
- final operating entity and terms;
- final utility terms;
- privacy and data processing review;
- treasury and governance approval;
- independent security audit;
- risk disclosure updates;
- clear jurisdictional limitations;
- verified source and deployment records;
- updated claims registry.

This list is not approval to make value claims.

## Banned Public Claims

Do not claim or imply:

- Do not claim TIDE has value.
- Do not say buy TIDE.
- Do not say invest in TIDE.
- Do not promise a guaranteed benefit.
- Do not claim an active hotel benefit.
- Do not claim hotel ownership.
- Do not claim revenue share.
- Do not claim an exchange listing.
- Do not say mainnet live.
- Do not say independently audited.
- Do not claim price growth.
- Do not claim APY, yield, staking, or passive income.

## Counsel-Required Claims

Counsel review is required before public statements about:

- real-world utility;
- discounts, memberships, bookings, or access;
- partner campaign work that requires legal review before any public claim;
- data collection from participants;
- transfer campaigns;
- redemption;
- exchange access;
- treasury use;
- mainnet deployment;
- paid services connected to TIDE.
