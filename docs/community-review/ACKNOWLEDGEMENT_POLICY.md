# Community Reviewer Acknowledgement Policy

Public credit requires both:

1. a real, referenceable contribution to the exact review candidate; and
2. explicit consent to the displayed name or handle and contribution reference.

Outreach, a reply, expressed interest, session attendance, private reporting, or review in progress does not by itself permit public acknowledgement.

## Required Evidence

- matching outreach record;
- candidate commit;
- public contribution URL or privacy-safe private reference;
- consent recorded as `true` with a timestamp;
- exact approved display name or handle;
- disclosure status that does not expose a sensitive unresolved vulnerability.

Consent may be withdrawn. Remove future public attribution after a verified request while preserving non-identifying project records where operationally required. Never expose private report details, personal contact information, secrets or unrelated personal data.

Acknowledgement is credit for a contribution, not an endorsement, employment relationship, partnership, formal audit conclusion or project-safety claim.
