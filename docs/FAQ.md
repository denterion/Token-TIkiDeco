# TikiDeco FAQ

## What is TikiDeco / TIDE?

TikiDeco / TIDE is a public Ethereum Sepolia testnet prototype for a future hospitality-linked token concept connected to the TikiDeco Miami Beach idea.

## Is TIDE live on mainnet?

No. TIDE is currently deployed on Ethereum Sepolia, which is a test network. Sepolia tokens have no market value.

## Is this an investment offer?

No. The current project is a technical prototype and public demonstration. It is not an offer to sell securities, investment advice, legal advice, or a promise of profit.

## What can people inspect today?

People can inspect the public GitHub repository, verified Sepolia smart contracts, tokenomics, white paper drafts, roadmap, deployment records, and security policy.

## What does the token do today?

The current token contract provides a fixed-supply ERC-20 compatible token, treasury allocation, pause controls, report hash publishing, and integration with a vesting vault.

## What is the future utility idea?

Potential future utility may include community rewards, loyalty campaigns, early access to hospitality-related experiences, and public transparency workflows. Any final utility model must be reviewed legally before a production launch.

## Can TIDE represent ownership in a hotel?

Not in the current prototype. Do not describe TIDE as hotel ownership, equity, debt, revenue share, or profit share unless a compliant legal structure is created and reviewed by securities counsel.

## How can I view TIDE in MetaMask?

Follow [`docs/METAMASK.md`](METAMASK.md). You must switch MetaMask to Ethereum Sepolia and import the token contract address.
