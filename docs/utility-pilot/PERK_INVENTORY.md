# TIDE Loyalty Pilot Perk Inventory

Status: planned inventory model. Not live. Sepolia-only. No sale, no stated monetary value, no mainnet deployment, and independent audit not started.

## Inventory Principles

Every pilot perk must be:

- limited by published inventory;
- conditional on eligibility review;
- non-cash;
- not redeemable for cash;
- not represented as having resale value;
- subject to cancellation and local-law restrictions;
- documented in a campaign report.

No perk creates ownership, revenue, property, financial return, booking, membership, or guaranteed hospitality rights.

## Category 1: Early RSVP Window

Description: eligible wallets may receive an earlier RSVP submission window for a selected community preview.

Required rules:

- no guarantee of acceptance;
- limited availability;
- no resale value;
- no cash redemption;
- cancellation allowed for legal, safety, staffing, weather, venue, or operational reasons;
- subject to local law and venue restrictions;
- manual review required before confirmation.

Operational notes:

- publish campaign ID, snapshot block, RSVP window, inventory, and dispute deadline;
- track accepted, waitlisted, rejected, and cancelled counts;
- publish a privacy-safe report after the campaign.

## Category 2: Priority Access To Selected Community Preview Events

Description: eligible wallets may receive priority review for a selected community preview event.

Required rules:

- no guarantee of access;
- limited availability;
- no resale value;
- no cash redemption;
- cancellation allowed for legal, safety, staffing, weather, venue, or operational reasons;
- subject to local law, age, venue, privacy, and conduct restrictions;
- staff override may reject or reassign requests.

Operational notes:

- priority review is not a paid service and not a financial benefit;
- participation may require separate event terms;
- if demand exceeds inventory, use the published allocation method.

## Category 3: Non-Cash Welcome Eligibility Where Legally Allowed

Description: eligible wallets may be considered for a small non-cash welcome item or experience where legally and operationally allowed.

Required rules:

- no guarantee of receipt;
- limited availability;
- no resale value;
- no cash redemption;
- cancellation allowed for legal, safety, staffing, supply, venue, or operational reasons;
- subject to local law, tax, venue, shipping, age, and availability restrictions;
- manual review required before confirmation.

Operational notes:

- do not describe the item as compensation, yield, reward income, or financial return;
- publish inventory limits before the campaign opens;
- report aggregate fulfilled/cancelled counts after the campaign.
