# TIDE Testnet Allocation Policy

Status: planned policy for a Sepolia-only pilot. No campaign is live until a campaign notice and allocation report are published.

## Sepolia-Only Test Allocation

The TIDE Loyalty Pilot may use Sepolia TIDE balances to test an eligibility and manual-review workflow. This policy applies only to Ethereum Sepolia and does not create a mainnet claim, sale, payment, redemption, or active hospitality benefit.

## No Sale / No Value / No Mainnet

- TIDE is not offered for sale.
- TIDE has no stated monetary value.
- TIDE is not deployed on mainnet.
- Independent audit has not started.
- Pilot eligibility has no cash value, resale value, or transfer of guest rights.

## No Guaranteed Benefit

Passing a read-only eligibility check does not guarantee a benefit, booking, event entry, discount, welcome item, or hospitality service. Any pilot outcome is limited, conditional, manually reviewed, and subject to published campaign rules.

## Eligibility Criteria

Each campaign must publish:

- campaign ID;
- canonical token address;
- Sepolia chain ID;
- minimum Sepolia TIDE threshold;
- snapshot block or approved live-check window;
- request window;
- maximum inventory or request cap;
- duplicate-wallet policy;
- manual-review criteria;
- dispute window;
- privacy notice;
- report publication target.

## Anti-Spam Rules

The pilot may reject or manually review requests involving:

- duplicate wallets;
- repeated submissions;
- automated request patterns;
- inconsistent wallet-control proof;
- attempts to bypass inventory limits;
- abusive, unsafe, or deceptive behavior.

## Wallet And Transaction Safety

- No private keys or seed phrases may be requested.
- No transaction signing may be requested.
- No token approvals, transfers, fees, or booking confirmations may be requested.

## Allocation Report Process

After a campaign closes, a privacy-safe report should be published with:

- campaign ID;
- token address and chain ID;
- snapshot block or live-check window;
- threshold;
- total requests;
- eligible requests;
- rejected requests by broad reason category;
- available inventory;
- allocation method;
- disputes and corrections;
- no private participant data;
- report hash.

The report must not describe TIDE as sold, value-bearing, mainnet live, independently audited, or connected to guaranteed hospitality benefits.
