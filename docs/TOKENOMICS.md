# TikiDeco Tokenomics

This document defines the starter allocation model for `100,000,000 TIDE`.

The goal is to keep the token transparent, predictable, and connected to a real project without making uncontrolled promises about returns, ownership, or revenue share.

## Allocation

| Bucket | Percent | Amount | Vesting | Purpose |
| --- | ---: | ---: | --- | --- |
| Treasury operations | 20% | 20,000,000 TIDE | Unlocked to treasury/multisig | Permits, legal setup, early project operations, and reserves for launch expenses. |
| Team and advisors | 15% | 15,000,000 TIDE | 12-month cliff, then linear vesting over 36 months | Long-term alignment for founders, operators, and advisors. |
| Strategic partners | 10% | 10,000,000 TIDE | 6-month cliff, then linear vesting over 24 months | Architecture, hospitality, development, and brand partnerships. |
| Community rewards | 20% | 20,000,000 TIDE | Linear distribution over 48 months | Community campaigns, referrals, education, and guest loyalty rewards. |
| Future hotel perks | 15% | 15,000,000 TIDE | Linear distribution over 48 months | Room upgrades, early booking access, events, and brand experiences. |
| Strategic reserve | 20% | 20,000,000 TIDE | Held by treasury/multisig | Unexpected costs, future liquidity planning, and governance-approved uses. |

Total: `100%`

## Operating Model

1. Deploy `TikiDecoToken`.
2. Full supply is minted to `TREASURY_ADDRESS`.
3. Deploy `TikiDecoVestingVault`.
4. Treasury approves the vault for any allocation that needs vesting.
5. Owner creates vesting schedules for team, partners, community rewards, and future hotel perks.

## Legal Boundary

This tokenomics plan is a project-management structure, not an offering document. Do not market TIDE as equity, debt, revenue share, profit share, or an investment return without securities counsel and the correct legal structure.
