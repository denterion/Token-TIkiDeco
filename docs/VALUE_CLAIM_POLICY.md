# Value Claim Policy

Status: `not approved for value claims`

This policy is a public-communications and release-engineering control. It is not legal advice, not legal approval, not a promise, not a value statement, and not an offer.

TIDE is currently an Ethereum Sepolia prototype. TIDE is not offered for sale, has no stated monetary value, is not deployed on mainnet, and has not completed an independent audit.

## Definitions

| Term | Project meaning |
| --- | --- |
| No stated monetary value | The project does not assign, endorse, publish, imply, or support a monetary value for TIDE. |
| Market price | Any third-party market activity, if it ever exists, is not project-endorsed value unless counsel-approved project materials say otherwise. |
| Utility value | Operational usefulness in a defined pilot is not financial return, cash value, resale value, or investment value. |
| Business value | Better reporting, loyalty workflow clarity, or operations learning is business-process value, not investor return. |

## Banned Phrases

Do not use these phrases as project claims:

- Do not say buy TIDE.
- Do not say invest in TIDE.
- Do not say TIDE has value.
- Do not promise guaranteed profit.
- Do not claim price growth.
- Do not claim passive income.
- Do not claim APY.
- Do not claim yield.
- Do not claim staking rewards.
- Do not claim revenue share.
- Do not claim hotel ownership.
- Do not claim exchange listing.
- Do not say mainnet live.
- Do not say independently audited.

## Counsel-Required Phrases

The following require counsel review before public use:

- real-world utility;
- redemption;
- discount;
- membership;
- transferable access;
- partner campaign that requires legal review;
- paid access;
- token distribution;
- liquidity;
- listing;
- value;
- mainnet.

## Conditions Before Value-Related Public Statements

No value-related public statement may be made unless all are complete:

| Condition | Current status |
| --- | --- |
| Legal counsel memo approves the exact language | not approved |
| Independent smart-contract audit is complete and findings are triaged | not approved |
| Entity, IP, treasury, tax, and accounting treatment are documented | requires review |
| Privacy and operational terms are approved if user data or guest workflows exist | requires review |
| Public communications review approves the exact statement | requires review |
| Claims registry and site checks are updated to enforce the approved language | requires review |

Until then, public language must say: no sale, no stated monetary value, no mainnet deployment, not independently audited, no equity, debt, revenue, property, hotel ownership, or return rights.
