# TikiDeco Risk Disclosure

This document summarizes key risks and communication boundaries for the TikiDeco / TIDE prototype.

## Prototype Risk

TIDE is currently a Sepolia testnet prototype. Sepolia tokens are not real assets and have no market value.

## Regulatory Risk

If TIDE is marketed or structured as an investment, profit share, revenue share, debt instrument, ownership right, or expectation of returns from the efforts of others, it may be treated as a regulated securities product.

## Business Risk

The future TikiDeco Miami Beach hospitality concept may change, be delayed, or never launch. The current token prototype does not guarantee hotel development, permits, financing, construction, operations, or commercial success.

## Smart Contract Risk

The contracts have local tests and verified Sepolia source code, but they have not received an independent audit. Bugs, configuration mistakes, or integration risks may exist.

## Treasury Risk

The treasury currently receives the full initial token supply on Sepolia. Any future production treasury should use stronger governance, ideally a multisig wallet such as Safe.

## Communication Boundary

Do not describe TIDE as guaranteed profit, hotel equity, real estate ownership, revenue share, dividend rights, debt claims, regulator-approved, or guaranteed to launch on mainnet.

Use clear wording: public testnet prototype, hospitality-linked token concept, community and transparency layer, and future utility subject to legal review.
