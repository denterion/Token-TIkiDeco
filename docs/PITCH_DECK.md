# TikiDeco Pitch Deck

## 1. Title

TikiDeco / TIDE

A transparent Ethereum token layer for a future Miami Beach hospitality concept.

## 2. The Idea

TikiDeco combines art deco hospitality, tropical Miami Beach energy, and blockchain transparency. The token is designed as a public coordination layer around community rewards, future hotel perks, transparent reporting, and long-term project alignment.

## 3. Why A Token

TIDE can support:

- public project transparency
- community participation
- loyalty and reward campaigns
- future hotel-related access and perks
- disciplined allocation through vesting

It should not be marketed as profit share, equity, debt, or guaranteed appreciation.

## 4. What Exists Today

- ERC-20 compatible token deployed on Sepolia
- Vesting vault deployed on Sepolia
- Fixed supply of `100,000,000 TIDE`
- Treasury receives the full supply
- Owner and treasury roles are separated
- On-chain report hash publishing
- Tokenomics and white paper drafts
- Automated tests and CI

## 7. Safety Model

- Fixed supply
- No public mint
- Two-step ownership transfer
- Pause controls
- Vesting with reentrancy protection
- Accidental ETH rejection
- Tests for core token and vault behavior
