## Suggested Public Message

TikiDeco / TIDE is live on Ethereum Sepolia as a public testnet prototype. The project includes a fixed-supply ERC-20 compatible token, a vesting vault, transparent tokenomics, multilingual white paper drafts, and on-chain report hash support. It is not a mainnet launch or investment offering.

## What People Can Check

- The token and vesting vault are deployed on Sepolia.
- The treasury address holds the full `100,000,000 TIDE` supply.
- The owner address controls admin functions.
- The GitHub repository includes tests, CI, deployment records, and docs.

## What Not To Say

Do not say:

- "Invest and earn"
- "Guaranteed returns"
- "Hotel ownership token"
- "Revenue share"
- "SEC-approved"
- "Mainnet launch is guaranteed"

Use:

- "public testnet prototype"
- "hospitality-linked token concept"
- "community and transparency layer"
- "future utility subject to legal review"
