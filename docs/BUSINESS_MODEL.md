# TikiDeco Business Model

Status: planning document. TikiDeco / TIDE is currently an Ethereum Sepolia prototype. TIDE is not offered for sale, has no stated monetary value, is not deployed on mainnet, and has not completed an independent audit.

## Summary

The intended business direction is hospitality operations plus transparency infrastructure. It is not token speculation.

TikiDeco explores whether public records, signed wallet verification, report hashes, and clear campaign rules can support future loyalty and access workflows for a hospitality concept.

## Real Business Value From Transparent Loyalty

Transparent loyalty can reduce ambiguity in eligibility workflows:

- campaign rules can be published before review starts;
- snapshot blocks can make eligibility timing clear;
- public reports can summarize outcomes;
- report hashes can help show that records were not silently changed;
- disputes can reference published rules.

This is operational value for a business process. It is not a claim that TIDE has financial value.

## Guest Acquisition

A future pilot may help understand whether community members are interested in transparent RSVP, preview, and non-cash welcome workflows.

Guest acquisition claims must remain limited to learning and feedback until real operations, legal review, privacy terms, and campaign reports exist.

## Community Engagement

Community engagement may include feedback, documentation review, issue reports, and pilot interest forms if privacy review allows data collection.

Engagement should not be framed as investment participation, price participation, or a route to guaranteed hospitality benefits.

## Counsel-Required Venue Campaigns

Partner or venue campaigns require legal review before any public claim or launch. Until then, this project should only describe such campaigns as possible future concepts, not confirmed relationships.

Any future campaign would need:

- written operating rules;
- local-law review;
- privacy review;
- inventory limits;
- cancellation rules;
- dispute process;
- public report format.

## Report Integrity

The project can create business value by publishing:

- deployment facts;
- role and Safe configuration;
- pilot campaign rules;
- pilot outcome reports;
- document hashes;
- correction records.

Report integrity helps readers verify process history. It does not prove financial value, active benefits, or legal compliance.

## Operational Limits

The current project does not have:

- active hotel benefits;
- confirmed hospitality operations;
- completed property claim;
- token sale;
- mainnet deployment;
- independent audit;
- approved real-world utility terms.

## Legal Review Requirements

External counsel review is required before:

- broader promotion of utility claims;
- collecting participant data;
- confirming a real-world hospitality benefit;
- launching venue or partner campaign work that requires legal review before any public claim;
- using TIDE in any paid, redemption, transfer, or exchange-related flow;
- discussing mainnet deployment.

Until those reviews are complete, public language should remain limited to Sepolia prototype, planned pilot documentation, and transparent review preparation.
