## Purpose

This report creates the first public transparency checkpoint for the TikiDeco / TIDE prototype. It records the current testnet deployment, verified contract addresses, tokenomics model, public documentation, website, and communication boundaries.

This report is not an investment memo, securities offering, hotel ownership document, revenue-share document, or promise of profit.

## Communication Boundary

TIDE should be described as:

- public testnet prototype
- hospitality-linked token concept
- community and transparency layer
- future utility subject to legal review

TIDE should not be described as:

- guaranteed profit
- hotel equity
- real estate ownership
- revenue share
- dividend right
- debt claim
- regulator-approved product
- guaranteed mainnet launch

## Mainnet Gate

Before any mainnet decision, the project should complete:

- legal review
- independent smart-contract audit
- treasury governance plan
- owner and treasury multisig plan
- final utility language
- public risk disclosures
